Who We Are

Romche Company is a trusted transport and logistics company specializing in safe and efficient movement of goods and products across Kenya and neighbouring East African countries.

We are committed to professionalism, timely deliveries, cargo safety and excellent customer service for both businesses and individuals.

Our experienced drivers and logistics team ensure smooth transportation operations with reliability and efficiency at every stage.

Our Mission

To provide secure, efficient and dependable logistics and transportation services while maintaining high customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To become one of the leading transport and logistics companies in East Africa through professionalism, innovation and reliability.
